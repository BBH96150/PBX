# Call History and Recordings

Every call your workspace makes or receives is logged automatically. The call history (CDRs — Call Detail Records) lives under the **Calls** tab.

## Browse and filter

1. Open the **Calls** tab.
2. Use the filter bar to narrow results:
   - **Direction** — inbound, outbound, internal, or any.
   - **Search** — a number or name.
   - **From** / **To** — a date range.
3. Click **Filter**. Use **Clear** to reset.

Each row shows when the call started, its direction, the from/to parties, duration, billed seconds, disposition (for example **ANSWERED**), and hangup cause.

## Play and download recordings

If a call was recorded (because the extension has **Always record** on), the **Recording** column shows an inline player. You can play it, download it (**⬇**), or delete it (**✕**).

To record an extension's calls, turn on **Always record** in its [call-handling features](calling-forwarding-and-dnd.md).

## Add a note or tag to a call

In the **Note** column, type a short note or tag next to any call and click the check mark to save it. Notes are included in the CSV export.

## Export to CSV

Click **⬇ Export CSV** in the filter bar to download the filtered call log — including start time, direction, parties, durations, disposition, hangup cause, and notes.
