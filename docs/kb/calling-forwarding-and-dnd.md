# Call Forwarding, Do-Not-Disturb, and Recording

Each extension has call-handling settings that control what happens to its incoming calls. Admins set these on the extension's detail page; the extension's owner can set most of them from their [self-service area](self-service-portal.md).

## Where to find them

- **As an admin:** open the extension's detail page and use the **Features** card.
- **As the extension's owner:** open your [self-service area](self-service-portal.md) and use **Call handling**.

## The settings

- **Voicemail enabled** — send unanswered calls to voicemail. (Set up the box itself in [Voicemail](voicemail-setup.md).)
- **Do Not Disturb (DND)** — send all incoming calls straight to voicemail or your forward target.
- **Always record** (admin only) — record every call on this extension. Recordings appear with the call in the call history.
- **CF immediate** (Forward all calls) — forward every call to another extension or an outside number in +E.164 format.
- **CF busy** — forward only when you're already on a call.
- **CF no-answer** — forward only when you don't pick up.

For any forward field, enter an extension or a number like `+14155551234`. Leave it blank to turn that forward off.

## Save

Click **Save** (admin) or **Save settings** (self-service) to apply your changes.
