# Call recording

Record calls for training, quality, or compliance, and play them back from your
call history.

## Turn recording on for an extension

Recording is controlled per extension:

1. Go to the extension (from the tenant overview, or your own at
   **My extension**).
2. Enable **Recording**. New calls on that extension are recorded from then on.

> Recording is a call-handling feature alongside DND and call forwarding — see
> [Call forwarding, do-not-disturb, and recording](calling-forwarding-and-dnd.md).

## Find and play recordings

Recorded calls appear in your **call history** (CDRs):

1. Open **Calls** for the tenant.
2. A call that was recorded shows a recording you can **play**, **download**,
   or **delete** inline.

See [Call history and recordings](reports-call-history.md) for filtering and CSV
export.

## Things to know

- **Storage:** recordings are stored on the platform and streamed back through
  the portal — they are not exposed publicly.
- **Deleting:** removing a recording from the call record deletes the audio.
- **Legal:** recording laws vary by region — some require you to notify or get
  consent from the parties on the call. Make sure your usage complies with the
  rules where you and your callers are located. Consider an announcement or IVR
  greeting that informs callers calls may be recorded.
