# The Broadcast App: Voice Blasts and Live Push-to-Talk

The Broadcast app is a phone-friendly web app for sending pages to a paging group — either a pre-recorded voice blast or a live, hold-to-talk announcement. You can install it to your phone's home screen so it opens like a native app.

## Open it

1. Sign in at **https://pbx.tendpos.com/admin**.
2. Go to **https://pbx.tendpos.com/admin/broadcast**, or click **Open broadcaster** from the **Messaging ▾ → Paging / PTT** page.

> The app needs a workspace context. If it says so, open it from a workspace login (or use the workspace switcher in the portal) and reload.

## Record and send a voice blast

1. Make sure the **Record & send** tab is selected.
2. Choose the target group under **Broadcast to**.
3. Tap the round **Record** button and speak. The timer and level meter show it's capturing.
4. Tap again to stop. Use the player to review, **Discard** to redo, or
5. Tap **Broadcast** to send. The recording plays to every member of the group.

## Live push-to-talk

1. Select the **Live** tab.
2. Choose the group under **Broadcast to** and pick the extension to **Talk as**.
3. Tap **Go live** to connect.
4. **Hold** the button and speak; **release** to end the page.

> Live push-to-talk needs an extension assigned to your account and a configured server. Recorded voice blasts work without one.

## Install it to your home screen

Because it's a Progressive Web App (PWA), you can add it to your phone's home screen for one-tap access:

- **iPhone/iPad (Safari):** open the broadcast page, tap the **Share** button, then **Add to Home Screen**.
- **Android (Chrome):** open the broadcast page, tap the **⋮** menu, then **Add to Home screen** (or **Install app**).

It then launches full-screen like an app, titled "Paging."
