# SMS Messages (Preview)

Your workspace can hold text-message conversations tied to your phone numbers, under **Messaging ▾ → Messages**.

> **Preview feature.** The system stores conversations and queues outbound messages, but carrier delivery isn't enabled yet — sent messages stay **queued** until an SMS trunk is configured for your workspace. Talk to your account contact about enabling SMS delivery.

## See conversations

Go to **Messaging ▾ → Messages**. Each row shows who the conversation is with, which of your numbers it's on, the last message, and when. Click **Open →** to view the full thread.

## Send a message

1. In **New message**, choose **From (your number)** — one of your phone numbers.
2. Enter the **To** number in E.164 format (for example `+14155551234`).
3. Type your **Message**.
4. Click **Queue message**.

You need at least one phone number on your workspace to send.
