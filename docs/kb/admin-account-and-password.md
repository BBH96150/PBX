# Your Account: Profile, Password, and Security

Manage your own login from the account area, regardless of your role.

## Open your account

1. Sign in at **https://pbx.tendpos.com/admin**.
2. Go to **Account** (at `/admin/security/account`). From here you can reach **Account**, **2FA**, **Linked SSO**, and **Active sessions**.

## Update your display name

1. On the **Profile** card, edit your **Display name**.
2. Click **Save profile**.

## Change your password

1. On the **Change password** card, enter your **Current password**.
2. Enter and confirm a **New password** (at least 8 characters).
3. Click **Change password**. You stay signed in on this device.

## Forgot your password?

On the sign-in page, use **Forgot password** to receive a reset link by email, then set a new password from that link.
