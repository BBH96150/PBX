# Reports and the Live Call View

Beyond the detailed call log, your workspace has two at-a-glance views: a live view of calls happening right now, and summary reports.

## Live call view

1. Open the **Live** tab.
2. You'll see calls currently in progress, refreshing automatically.
3. If a call needs to be ended, use the **Hang up** control next to it.

This is useful for spotting stuck calls or confirming a test call connected.

## Reports

1. Open the **Reports** tab.
2. Review summary statistics for your workspace's call activity — volume and related metrics.

For row-by-row detail, filtering, recordings, and CSV export, use the [Calls history](reports-call-history.md) instead.
