Meet-me conference bridges are dial-in audio rooms. Give a room a dialable number; anyone who dials it is prompted for the PIN (if set) and joined into the conference.

  • Member PIN — required to enter as a participant (optional; leave blank for an open room).
  • Moderator PIN — joins with moderator privileges; the conference ends when the last moderator leaves.
