What's a trunk?

A SIP trunk connects this PBX to the public phone network (PSTN) through a carrier like CallCentric, Telnyx, or Bandwidth. Once a trunk is registered, your extensions can call real phone numbers and receive calls on the DIDs you've purchased from that carrier.

CallCentric format help
  • Main account: 11 digits starting with 1777 (e.g. 17771234567).
  • Sub-account / extension: main + * + sub-number (e.g. 17771234567*100). The asterisk is required.
  • Not your DID or your CallCentric login email — those won't work.
  • Find the right value in the CallCentric portal under My Account → Account Info.
  • The SIP password is the one shown on the same page (separate from your web login).
Advanced
