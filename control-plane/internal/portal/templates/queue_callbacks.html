Queue callbacks let a caller keep their place in line: instead of holding, they request a callback and the system calls them back and drops them into the queue to reach the next agent.

  • Callers opt in by reaching *8<queue number> (point a queue's overflow / timeout or an IVR option at it).
  • Call now dials the caller back through this tenant's trunk and transfers them into the queue. Auto-dial also runs in the background (every ~20s, up to 3 attempts).
