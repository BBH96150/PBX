# Trunk-Down Alerts and the Daily Digest

Two built-in email notifications help you stay on top of your phone system without watching the portal: trunk-down alerts and a daily call summary.

## Choose who gets alerts

1. Go to **Numbers ▾ → Trunks**.
2. At the top, set the **Alert email** under **Trunk alert recipient**.
3. Leave it blank to email your workspace admins automatically; enter an address to send alerts somewhere specific (for example a shared inbox).
4. Click **Save**.

## Trunk-down alerts

When a carrier trunk loses registration (and again when it recovers), an email goes to your trunk-alert recipient so you know your inbound/outbound calling may be affected. No setup beyond choosing the recipient above.

## Daily summary email (digest)

The daily digest emails yesterday's call summary to your trunk-alert recipients each morning.

1. Open the **Reports** tab.
2. Find **Daily summary email** and click **Turn on** (or **Turn off**).

## If alert emails aren't arriving

Email delivery depends on the platform's mail configuration. See the email section in [Troubleshooting & FAQ](troubleshooting-faq.md), and confirm the recipient address is correct.
