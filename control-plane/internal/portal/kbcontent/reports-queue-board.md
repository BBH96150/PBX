# The Live Queue Board

The queue board is a real-time wallboard for your call queues — see who's waiting and how your agents are doing, refreshing automatically.

## Open it

From your workspace, open the queue board / live queues page (the `queues-live` page for your workspace under `/admin/tenants/<your-workspace>/queues-live`).

## What it shows

- Callers currently waiting in each queue and how long they've been holding.
- Agent status and activity across your queues.

The board updates on its own, so you can leave it open on a screen for the team.
