# Invite Your Team and Set Roles

Add coworkers to your workspace by sending them an invitation. They set their own password when they accept. Find this under **Admin ▾ → Team**.

## Invite a teammate

1. Go to **Admin ▾ → Team**.
2. In **Invite a teammate**, enter their **Email**.
3. Choose a **Role**:
   - **user** — everyday access, confined to their own self-service area (their assigned extension, voicemail, softphone).
   - **tenant_admin** — full management of the workspace.
4. Click **Send invite**.

If email is configured they'll receive the invitation automatically. If not, the accept link is shown on the page so you can hand it to them directly (in chat or text).

## Track outstanding invites

The **Outstanding invites** table shows each invite's email, role, status (**pending**, **accepted**, or **revoked**), and expiry. Invites expire after 7 days.

## Revoke an invite

Click **Revoke** next to a pending invite to cancel it before it's accepted.

## Give a user self-service access to an extension

Inviting someone creates their account; to let them manage a specific phone, assign them as the **owner** of an extension. See [Create and manage extensions](extensions-create-and-manage.md).
