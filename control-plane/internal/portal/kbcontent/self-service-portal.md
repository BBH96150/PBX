# Manage Your Own Phone (Self-Service)

You don't need to be an administrator to manage your own extension. If an admin has assigned an extension to your account, you get a personal self-service area for forwarding, do-not-disturb, and voicemail.

## Open your self-service area

1. Sign in at **https://pbx.tendpos.com/admin**.
2. You'll land on **My extensions** (the `/me` page). If you don't, go there directly at **https://pbx.tendpos.com/admin/me**.
3. Click **Manage →** next to the extension you want to change.

> If you see "No extension is assigned to your account yet," ask your administrator to assign one to you.

## Change your call handling

On your extension's **Call handling** card you can:

1. Turn **Do not disturb** on or off — this sends incoming calls straight to voicemail or your forward target.
2. **Forward all calls to** another extension or an outside number in +E.164 format (for example `+14155551234`). Leave it blank to turn forwarding off.
3. **Forward when busy to** — used when you're already on a call.
4. **Forward when no answer to** — used when you don't pick up.
5. Toggle **Voicemail enabled**.
6. Click **Save settings**.

## Listen to your voicemail

On the **Voicemail** card you can play each message right in the browser, see who called and when, and delete messages you no longer need. New messages are marked with a **new** tag.

## See your recent calls

The **Recent calls** card lists your latest inbound, outbound, and internal calls with direction, the other party, and duration.
