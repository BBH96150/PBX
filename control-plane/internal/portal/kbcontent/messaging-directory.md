# Directory / Contacts

The directory is your workspace's shared contact list — names, numbers, companies, and notes you can search and export. Find it under **Messaging ▾ → Directory**.

## Add a contact

1. Go to **Messaging ▾ → Directory**.
2. In **Add a contact**, fill in:
   - **Name** (required).
   - **Number** (required) — a full number like `+14155551234` or an internal extension.
   - **Company** (optional).
   - **Notes** (optional).
3. Click **Add contact**.

## Search and export

- Use the search box to filter by name, number, or company.
- Click **⬇ CSV** to download the (filtered) list as a spreadsheet.

## Remove a contact

Click **Remove** on a contact's row and confirm.
